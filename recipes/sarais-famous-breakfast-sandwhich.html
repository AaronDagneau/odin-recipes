Sarai's Famous Breakfast Sandwhich

Start your day off right with Sarai's Famous Breakfast Sandwhich! This easy to craft masterpiece will replace that boring milk and cereal or microwavable oatmeal. You will never want to go back to having normal breakfasts again, and for the better!

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Teaspoons Water
  • 1 Slice Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 Slices Thick Cut Bacon, Cut in Half
  • 2 Slices Sourdough Bread
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter
  • Mayonaise (Optional)
  • Tabasco Sause to Taste (Optional)

Steps

  1. Heat pan on stovetop and cook bacon on medium heat until crisp
  2. While bacon is cooking, crack eggs into a bowl and mix salt, pepper, and water
  3. When the bacon is done cooking, pour the egg mixture into the heated pan. Spread the egg mixture on the pan and cook as a solid piece. Once egg is firm, flip to other side. Once fully cooked, fold up the egg so that it will fit on a pice of bread.
  4. Lightly spread mayo on the interior sides of the bread, and assemble the sandwhich using the two slices of bread, cooked bacon and egg, and slice of cheddar cheese. Turn the heat down to low.
  5. In the heated pan, melt some butter before placeing the sadwhich in the pan. Allow the bread to become toasted to a golden color before flipping. Allow other side of sandwhich to also become toasted. By this time, the cheese should also have melted well.
  6. Apply Tobasco sause (optional) and enjoy the best easy homemade sandwhich to exist!
