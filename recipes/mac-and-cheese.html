Aaron's Delectable Hotdog Mac and Cheese

One of Aaron's go-to quick meals is this delicious mac and cheese hotdog combo! The flavors of orriginal Kraft mac and cheese partnered with premium 100% beef hotdogs is to die for. This is a perfect quick meal after getting off of work or home from school. This recipe is so easy that even children can make it for themselves!

Ingredients

  • 1 Box Kraft Mac and Cheese
  • 3 100% All Beef Premium Hotdogs
  • 1/4 Cup Milk
  • 1 3/4 Cup Water
  • 4 Tablespoons Butter
  • 1 Stem Cilantro
  • Pepper to Taste

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook the pasta for 8 minutes.
  2. While the water is boiling and pasta is cooking, cut the hotdogs into dimes and cook with a little oil in a pan on the stovetop untill slightly crisp and brown.
  3. Once pasta is done cooking, strain the water, add the butter, milk, and kraft cheese mix and mix well.
  4. Chop the cilantro and add both the hotdog dimes and cilantro to the mac and cheese and mix.
  5. Add pepper to taste (optional).
